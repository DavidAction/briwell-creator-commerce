Current Workflow

1. Creator Intake

Manual or approved-provider creator/video/comment imports for MX, PE, EC.

2. AI Analysis

Dry-run Gemini adapter validates profile, comment, and final review schemas.

3. Score Handoff

AI outputs become scoring dimensions. Final score is system-calculated.

4. Campaign Ranking

Latest creator analysis ranks eligible candidates by score and risk.

5. DM Draft

Spanish DM drafts are generated for claims review.

6. Human Approval

Passed drafts can be approved for manual send. Sending is not automated.

Main API Endpoints

Area Endpoint What You Can Test
Health GET /health Server status
AI POST /ai/dry-run Dry-run Gemini-style analysis output
Scoring POST /analysis-jobs/run-creator-score-handoff Convert AI analysis into score inputs and calculated final score
Campaign GET /campaigns/{campaign_id}/candidates Rank eligible creators for a campaign
Outreach POST /outreach/{creator_id}/generate-dm Generate Spanish DM draft with claims review required
Campaign Outreach POST /campaigns/{campaign_id}/outreach-drafts Prepare selected campaign candidates for DM draft and claims review
Compliance POST /outreach/claims-check Check DM text for disallowed, medical, guaranteed, or review-required claims
Approval POST /outreach/review-decision Record approve, revision, or reject decision before any manual send
Execution POST /outreach/status-transition Record manual send, reply, negotiation, acceptance, and delivery status
Tracking POST /performance/snapshots Record campaign performance metrics from approved sources
Settlement POST /settlements/contracts Record deliverables and compensation terms
Ops GET /ops/readiness Check production readiness gates
